So What Causes A Man To Want To Play The Bagpipes?

Well I'll tell you. There Were Four Main Things That Influenced My Choice:

  1. Every other instrument is boring. Everyone plays the piano, and everyones
    sister plays the violin, but the bagpipes are totally unique

  2. I used to play the piano, but I got sick of it. My mother, unbending as she
    is, said "You gotta play something!" so I chose an intrument that always
    caught my eye when I was near it.

  3. My family comes from Scotland, and I thought it would be a cool way to
    connect with the roots. I hope I have some Scottish grandfather
    looking at me in heaven playing the instrument of his homeland. And I
    hope he looks at me and says, in a thick accent "Good Lad".

  4. I genuinly love the sound. Whenever I play it around new people, they often
    laugh for a minute at the whole rediculus scene. But after a while, they start
    to listen to the sounds; the mourning and the joy of this strang device. It
    connects with peoples hearts, and that's worth it to me.
